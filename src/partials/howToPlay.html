How to Play

Match identical objects and clear every level

  • Search Through 3D Items

    Find three identical objects hidden on the board.

  • Create Triple Matches

    Collect matching items to remove them instantly.

  • Clear All Objects

    Complete levels by organizing the entire puzzle space.

  • Use Helpful Tools

    Boosters and hints support difficult puzzle stages.

  • Unlock New Object Themes

    Progress through increasingly varied visual collections.
