About the game

A modern 3D puzzle experience built around satisfying object matching. We took the old-school matching game concept and gave it a 3D makeover - now you're hunting through cluttered scenes to spot three identical items. It's actually pretty good for training your eye and focus, but it won't stress you out - perfect for playing on the bus or whatever. Everything feels smooth to touch, the objects look great, and you'll want to keep playing to see what's next. The overall mood should feel colorful, clean, and mentally refreshing.
